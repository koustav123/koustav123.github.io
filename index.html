About Me

I am a Lead AI/ML engineer in Mastercard, training and deploying ML models at scale. Previously, I worked for Accenture as a research scientist.

I have a background in Computer Vison and Natural Language Processing. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from Trinity College Dublin, and a research masters from IIIT Hyderabad, India.
